## Tone Rules for Positioning Content

[How do you talk about competitors and the forces you're against?]

- Name the force, not the person
- Be decisive, not bitter — "I call bullshit" is confident, "they're all frauds" is bitter
- Every competitive claim should be backed by a story or proof point
- Invite thinking, don't demand agreement — state your position and move on
- Keep it proportional — a quick contrarian Note vs. a developed standalone post are different scopes
