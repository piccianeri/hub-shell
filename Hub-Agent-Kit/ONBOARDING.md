# Hub Agent Kit — Onboarding Questionnaire

> **What this is**: Answer these questions so Claude can generate your three research profiles — the context files that make every agent work for YOUR business specifically.
>
> **Time needed**: 30-60 minutes
>
> **The rule**: Be specific and honest. "I help entrepreneurs" gives you generic results. "I help women 40+ who run service businesses and want to stop trading time for money" gives you agents that actually know who they're talking to.
>
> When you're done, open Claude Code in your project and ask: "Read my ONBOARDING.md and generate my three research profiles."

## What Gets Generated

**business-context.md** — Business overview, primary revenue streams, target audience (with avatar), platforms and channels, competitive positioning. This is the file every agent reads first.

**content-strategy.md** — Your content pillars (3-5 of them, based on your answers), voice guidelines, language patterns, what to avoid, publishing schedule. Agents use this to ensure research aligns with your content direction.

**competitor-watchlist.md** — The 5-7 people from your list, formatted as structured profiles with URLs and strategic context. This is what powers competitive analysis — the agents need to know who to watch.

---

*Update your profiles whenever your business meaningfully shifts. The questionnaire is a starting point — a living document is better than a perfect one.*
